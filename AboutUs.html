Planning a hike, climb or surf?

Great!

Use our website to stay safe during your adventure! Share your intended route and estimated time of return with friends or family. Log in and fill in the form to plan a trip and choose your emergency contact. We will email your friend and tell them where you're planning to go. If you don't come back in time, we will alarm your friend. They can try to contact you or call a rescue team. Thanks to the route you uploaded they will know where to search for you.

This website was inspired by tragic stories of travelers who lost their life in the wild, unable to find their way home. Their stories would have ended differently had the help arrived in time. Unfortunately, nobody knew where to look for them. Have fun, enjoy the wild and most of all, stay safe!
